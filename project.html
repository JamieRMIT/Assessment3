Description

Overview

Topic

This project is to help people who need to display information on their vehicles and to do it quickly and easily by using a colour E-Ink display partnered with an Arduino Nano. The displays will have two separate ways of being displayed. For cars, they can use window mounts for both the front and rear windscreens, these can also be used separately. For example, being mounted only on the rear windscreen or number plate mounts so that the displays can be mounted next to the number plates on the front or back of the vehicles. For trucks and other vehicles, they can be mounted on the back of trailers and the front of the cabin.

With the help of an iOS and Android application the user of these screens will be able to choose what is being displayed on the screens at any time. For example, the Australian learners or provisional plates can be displayed to help other drivers understand that the car is being driving by one of those drivers. These screens when used on their own or can also show novity signs like stick figure families or baby on board signs.

Motivation

Our motivation behind doing this E-Ink display project is to make it easier for drivers to display information to other drivers. At present we have a vast range of information being displayed on cars that could be integrated into a single solution to help make it easier for people to get the right information displayed on their vehicles.

Landscape

Right now, vehicle owners need to use decals, stick on plates like Ls or Ps, metal plates to show danger signs. However, there is nothing out in the wilderness that gives everything in one spot and help to reduce the waste when these signs or decals are no longer wanted.
